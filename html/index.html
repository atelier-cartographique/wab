4th WAB

The Wandering Arts Biennial is a research and production biennial that focuses on mobility, versatile production and presentation methods in the field of contemporary arts, and the nomadic practices of artists.

Organisation

  • WAB is a project by nadine vzw, and is supported by Vlaamse Gemeenschap, Vlaamse Gemeenschapscommissie, Mobiel Brussel.
  • -
  • 4th WAB runs from March to December 2020. Most of the public presentations take place in Brussels.
  • contact : info@wab.zone
  • WAB MEETING POINT : Nieuwbrug 3 Rue du Pont neuf, 1000 Brussels
    (open daily)
  • -
  • In 2014 nadine vzw created WAB to anticipate artists and creative thinkers using mobility, nomadism and temporality as an instrument or creative method in their artistic practice. WAB was conceived as an open platform where works can be shown, shared and communicated in an independent context. Each edition of the WAB is followed by a publication featuring contributions from all the participating artists.
