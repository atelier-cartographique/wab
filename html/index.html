WAB

The Wandering Arts Biennial is a research and production biennial that focuses on mobility, versatile production and presentation methods in the field of contemporary arts, and the nomadic practices of artists.

For this third edition Mira Sanders proposed a framework for reflection :

‘I propose to reflect upon the idea of “wandering along a limit(s)” (that can be political, historical, cultural, social, physical or geographical). The question that follows is, “how can you form (an) image(s) of a wandering along (a) limit(s)?”’

WAB reached out to other partners in the Brussels cultural field to join the platform and organise activities that nourish this year’s focus.

Organisation

  • WAB is a project by nadine vzw, and is supported by Vlaamse Gemeenschap, Vlaamse Gemeenschapscommissie, Mobiel Brussel, Cocof, Fédération Wallonie Bruxelles.
  • -
  • WAB 2018 runs from March to December. Most of the public presentations are concentrated around November and take place in Brussels.
  • contact : info@wab.zone
    WAB MEETING POINT : Nieuwbrug 3 Rue du Pont neuf, 1000 Brussels
    (open daily)
  • -
  • In 2014 nadine vzw created WAB to anticipate artists and creative thinkers using mobility, nomadism and temporality as an instrument or creative method in their artistic practice. WAB was conceived as an open platform where works can be shown, shared and communicated in an independent context. Each edition of the WAB is followed by a publication featuring contributions from all the participating artists.
